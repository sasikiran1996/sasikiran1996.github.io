Books, Books and more books

I read a lot of books and novels. I own a Kindle and like reading novels in it before going to bed. If I find a novel interesting, I don't sleep until I complete the whole book. My favourite genres are Fantasy and Historical Fiction. Some of my favourite authors are Rick Riordan, Dan Brown, Eoin Colfer, Jonathan Stroud, Christopher Paolini, Brandon Sanderson, Alexandre Dumas, Agatha Christie and last but not the least Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. (I started my reading career with Sherlock Holmes). I am always looking for new books to read and welcome any new suggestions.
